What is HTML Day?
HTML Day is an annual celebration of HTML. It's a day to gather IRL in places around the world to write and learn HTML.
What to bring?
Something to write HTML on: laptop (fully charged), mobile phone, or paper. Also something to sit on and maybe a snack and drink.
Who can join?
Anyone who wants to write HTML. Beginners are very, very welcome!
